Standardize and populate ALL devotionals in the database (past, present, and future).

--------------------------------------------------
STRUCTURE REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY FORMAT)
--------------------------------------------------

Each devotional must contain:

1. Devotional Message
   - Minimum 3 meaningful paragraphs
   - Theologically sound
   - Practical and encouraging
   - Paragraph spacing preserved

2. Prayer Points
   - Exactly 7
   - One per line
   - Spirit-filled and actionable

3. Faith Declarations
   - Exactly 5
   - One per line
   - Bold, identity-based

4. Christian Quotes
   - Exactly 2
   - One per line
   - Short and powerful

5. Prophetic Declaration
   - 1 strong paragraph
   - Confident, forward-looking
   - Maximum 6 sentences

--------------------------------------------------
PROCESSING LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

For EVERY devotional in the database:

IF:
- devotional message has fewer than 3 paragraphs
- prayerPoints count is less than 7
- faithDeclarations count is less than 5
- christianQuotes is null or empty
- propheticDeclaration is null or empty

THEN:
Regenerate ONLY the missing or incomplete sections.

IMPORTANT:
- If a section already meets the format standard, DO NOT overwrite it.
- Preserve tone and topic alignment with the existing title and scripture.
- Maintain devotional theme integrity.
