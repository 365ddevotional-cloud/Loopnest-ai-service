LESSON CONTENT:
OUTLINE POINT 1: Transformation Begins in the Mind
The battlefield of the believer is often mental.

OUTLINE POINT 2: Rejecting Worldly Patterns

OUTLINE POINT 3: Building a Scripture-Filled Mind
