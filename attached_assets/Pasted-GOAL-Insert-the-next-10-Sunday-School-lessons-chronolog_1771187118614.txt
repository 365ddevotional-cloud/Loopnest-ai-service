1) TITLE: The Blessing of Thanksgiving
DATE: 2027-10-03
SCRIPTURES: Psalm 103:1–5; 1 Thessalonians 5:18 (KJV)

SCRIPTURE TEXT (KJV):
Psalm 103:1 — Bless the Lord, O my soul: and all that is within me, bless his holy name.
1 Thessalonians 5:18 — In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you.

LESSON CONTENT:
OUTLINE POINT 1: Gratitude Awakens the Soul
Thanksgiving shifts focus from lack to blessing.

OUTLINE POINT 2: Thankfulness in All Circumstances
Gratitude is obedience, not emotion.

OUTLINE POINT 3: Thanksgiving as Worship
Giving thanks honors God publicly.

TEACHER EMPHASIS:
Encourage reflection.
Promote joyful gratitude.
Challenge complaining spirit.

APPLICATION:

1. Why is thanksgiving commanded?
2. How does gratitude affect faith?
3. What blocks thankfulness?
4. How can we cultivate gratitude daily?

PRAYER FOCUS:
Lord, teach us to be grateful.
Remove complaining from our hearts.

WEEKLY ASSIGNMENT:
Write a thanksgiving prayer daily.
